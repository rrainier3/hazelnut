Cre-animate.js

The easiest way to add onscoll animations to your website!

With Cre-animate.js you can quickly and easily add onscoll animations to any element on your website.
You have full control over the animation type, speed, delay, scroll offset and easing effect.
We've even included a Cre-animation Generator to generate the code for you.
Onscroll animations don't get any easier than this!

Adding animations to text and images has never been easier!







  • Adding animations to text and images has never been easier!

  • Choose from 11 Animation Types including Fade-In, Fade-Out, Slide-Down-From-Top, Slide-In-From-Right, Slide-Up-From-Bottom, Slide-In-From-Left, Scale-Up, Scale-Down, Rotate, Flip-Y-Axis and Flip-X-Axis.

  • Set the Animation Speed, Delay and Easing options.
    The Delay option is excellent when you want multiple items on the same line to be animated in at different times.

  • Choose the Animation Offset or Trigger Point which is the point on the page in which the animation will take place.

  • Best of all, we've included an Animation Generator which takes care of all the coding for you. Just select your desired animation options and hit "Generate Code". It doesn't get any easier than this!
